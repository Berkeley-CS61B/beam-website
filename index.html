Who We Are

Berkeley Engineers and Mentors (BEAM) fuses science education with mentorship by leading weekly hands-on science and engineering lessons at local elementary and middle schools. We provide accessible science education to our local communities in hopes of inspiring students of all socioeconomic backgrounds to pursue careers in STEM.

What We Do

You can find us at 16 elementary and middle schools in the Berkeley and Oakland area, teaching over 300 students a week with our engaging project based science and engineering lessons.
